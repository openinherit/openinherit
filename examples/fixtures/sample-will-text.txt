LAST WILL AND TESTAMENT

I, Margaret Elizabeth Brown, of 17 Elm Grove, Richmond, Surrey, TW9 1PQ, hereby revoke all former wills and testamentary dispositions and declare this to be my last will.

1. EXECUTORS
I appoint my son, Thomas James Brown, and my solicitor, David Arthur Pemberton of Pemberton & Associates, 4 King Street, Richmond, Surrey, TW9 2NA, to be the executors and trustees of this will.

2. SPECIFIC BEQUESTS
(a) I give my property at 17 Elm Grove, Richmond, Surrey, TW9 1PQ to my son Thomas James Brown absolutely.
(b) I give the sum of fifty thousand pounds (£50,000) to my daughter, Sarah Louise Brown-Walker.
(c) I give my collection of first-edition novels to my granddaughter, Emily Rose Walker.

3. RESIDUARY ESTATE
I give the residue of my estate to my children Thomas James Brown and Sarah Louise Brown-Walker in equal shares.

4. GUARDIANSHIP
Should my granddaughter Emily Rose Walker be under the age of eighteen at the date of my death, I appoint my son Thomas James Brown as her guardian.

5. FUNERAL WISHES
I wish to be cremated and my ashes scattered in Richmond Park.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF I have set my hand to this my last will this fourteenth day of March two thousand and twenty-six.

SIGNED by the said Margaret Elizabeth Brown )
in our joint presence and then by us in hers )    Margaret E. Brown

Witness 1: Patricia Ann Hughes, 22 Cedar Road, Richmond, TW9 3AB
Witness 2: Robert Michael Clarke, 8 Oak Lane, Richmond, TW9 4CD
